FAQ

Common questions about solar in Lucknow.

Is subsidy available for solar?

Residential subsidy eligibility depends on current government rules, system size and documentation. Prakash Energy can guide you during consultation.

How much maintenance is required?

Solar systems need periodic cleaning and basic performance checks. Most systems are low-maintenance when installed correctly.

What warranty can I expect?

Panel performance is typically designed for long-term output, while inverter and workmanship terms depend on the selected components and proposal.

How much can I save?

Savings depend on your bill, roof space, tariff, sanctioned load and consumption pattern. The calculator gives a quick estimate.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations can be completed quickly after survey, design approval, material readiness and required permissions.
